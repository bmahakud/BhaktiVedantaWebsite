    Our platform provides a sofware tool using which you can create your own test. If you are a teacher, you can create multiple tests for your students.

  • How DiCelpip is different from other platforms?

    We provide an exact Celpip like test experience through our tests for a much cheaper price! yes We guarantee that!

  • Who can use DiCelpip tools?

    Our tools are for anybody who wishes to take celpip tests or prepare tests based on his/her content. Once you have content , you can create tests. Moreover you are also directly practice our free practice tests.
